Smartphones deliver an endless stream of dopamine-triggering notifications and content, creating stimulus addiction patterns in developing brains that can affect focus, sleep, and emotional regulation for years to come.

Social media platforms amplify harmful social comparisons, exposing young minds to curated highlight reels that fuel anxiety, depression, and a distorted sense of self-worth during their most vulnerable years.
